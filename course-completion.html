Knowledge Check

This knowledge check covers the core disciplines from all eleven modules. For each question, select the answer that best reflects what you have learned — then use the results to identify any areas worth revisiting before you receive your certificate.

Tip

For each question, think about which module's core discipline it addresses and how the AI readiness thread connects that discipline to the business and AI outcomes the course has emphasized throughout. The strongest answers connect technical disciplines to the business and AI consequences they are designed to serve.

5. What is the primary role of survivorship rules in MDM?

9. Generative AI has raised the floor on data governance requirements in a specific way. Which of the following best describes that change?
