Types of Master Data

Explore the six core domains

Overview

Understand the six core domains of master data: Customer, Product, Financial, Partner & Supplier, Employee, and Location & Asset. Learn the unique characteristics of each domain.

Key Topics

  • Customer master data — accounts, contacts, hierarchies
  • Product master data — SKUs, attributes, categories
  • Financial master data — cost centers, ledgers, entities
  • Partner & supplier data — vendors, distributors
  • Employee master data — people, roles, locations
  • Location & asset data — sites, facilities, equipment
